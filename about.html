Our Story

Founded out of a passion for clean and healthy living spaces, JDDAMIE Cleans started as a small family-run business with a big mission: to transform every room we touch. Our founder believed that cleanliness should be accessible, reliable, and tailored to the unique needs of every client.

Over the years, we’ve grown into a full-service cleaning company trusted by hundreds of homeowners and businesses. Our attention to detail, eco-conscious practices, and professional integrity have earned us a reputation for excellence.
